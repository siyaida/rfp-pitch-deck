AI Agents

13 AI Agents, 5 Capabilities

GPT-5.2 for reasoning + QWEN 3.5 for native Arabic — orchestrated across the full RFP lifecycle.

Intelligent RFP Analysis [3 agents]

Extracts requirements, deadlines, budgets & evaluation criteria. Pulls metadata from Arabic & English docs. Scores relevancy against company capabilities.

Smart Go/No-Go Engine [1 agent]

Analyzes win probability, strategic value & resource fit. Assesses competition level and risk. Delivers clear GO/NO-GO with reasoning.

AI Question Generation & Routing [3 agents]

Generates targeted questions covering every RFP requirement. Auto-categorizes and routes to the right business unit. AI drafts answers using RAG.

Automated Proposal Generation [4 agents]

Plan → Map → Write → Fill pipeline. Synthesizes answers into professional narrative. Fills branded templates preserving AR/EN formatting.

AI Quality Assurance [2 agents: Document Reviewer + Compliance Checker]

Reviews for wrong-section content, broken text & duplicates. Checks compliance against all RFP requirements. Optimizes for clarity and persuasiveness.

13 specialized agents organized into 5 intelligent capabilities. Each capability has dedicated agents working together in pipelines — not standalone prompts.
